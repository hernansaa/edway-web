Pay annually and get 50% off your first year. From year two onward, you receive a 20% lifetime discount on the monthly rate. Cancel anytime with no penalty.

How do extra users work?

The Professional plan includes 2 users at $89/mo. Each additional user is $39/mo. The Enterprise plan includes unlimited users with no per-seat charges.

Yes. Upgrading to Enterprise is seamless—we scale your resources and activate new features immediately. No migration, no downtime.
