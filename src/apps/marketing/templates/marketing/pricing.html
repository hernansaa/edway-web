Common questions

Everything you need to know.

What's included in the setup fee?

Full server provisioning, data migration from your current system, team onboarding calls, and custom configuration. If you pay annually, we waive the entire $499 fee.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Upgrades are instant (we scale your server resources in real-time). Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. No migration required—it's the same instance.

What happens to my email deliverability?

You get your own dedicated IP address for email sending. No risk of being blacklisted because another tenant sent spam. Your reputation is yours alone.

Is there really no user limit?

Correct. Add your entire team, contractors, and new hires without your bill increasing. The only limit is your server's resources—which you can upgrade anytime.
